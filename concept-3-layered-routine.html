The anchor layer · Step 2 · Treat

What Mountain Glow actually does.

A concentrated golden serum built for mature skin — bakuchiol, sea buckthorn, and Vermont-pressed botanicals doing five jobs at once.

  • Softens fine linesBakuchiol, a gentle retinol alternative, smooths surface texture over time.
  • Eases deeper wrinklesPeptide complex supports collagen so expression lines look less etched-in.
  • Restores firmnessSea buckthorn and antioxidants help skin feel bouncier and more resilient.
  • Deep hydrationSqualane and glycerin lock in moisture without a heavy, greasy finish.
  • Golden-hour radianceBrightening botanicals leave skin lit-from-within, never glittery.
