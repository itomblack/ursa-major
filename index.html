Five UX directions

Tap any card to open the live prototype

These aren't visual variants — each is a different answer to the core challenge. Honest critique included so you can choose a direction before committing.

1Intent-gated

Progressive Reveal

Scroll / intent commitment

Opens as a pure Mountain Glow PDP. The regimen and builder stay hidden until the visitor signals intent — then unfold step by step. Locks the hero sale first, expands second.

Why it wins: truest to "they came for one thing." Watch: reveal must trigger on intent, not deep scroll, or AOV upside never surfaces on mobile.
Open prototype
2Personalized

Skin Consult

Diagnostic recommendation

A warm 3-question consult builds the regimen for you and auto-fills the bundle with a rationale per product. The bundle is an expert recommendation, not a grid.

Why it wins: highest personalization, lowest choice-paralysis. Watch: a quiz is friction — must be one-tap skippable so it never blocks the buy.
Open prototype
3Spatial

Layered Routine

Visual application stack

The builder is a spatial layering diagram — toggle steps and watch the routine physically stack in application order, Mountain Glow glowing at the anchor layer.

Why it wins: most memorable; makes products feel intentional, not cross-sold. Watch: must carry real education and degrade to a clean list at 390px.
Open prototype
4★Anchoring

Tiered Ritual

Good · better · best

Three curated tiers — Essential, Complete, Full Ritual — using anchoring and a decoy middle tier. Every tier is an on-ramp into the same live, customizable builder.

Why it wins: strongest pure-conversion play; decision is "how deep," not "yes/no." Watch: tiers must open into the builder so "build your routine" stays true.
Open prototype
5MNarrative

Founder Editorial

Story-led commerce

A long-form Emily Doyle essay. Each chapter earns a product its place; a sticky companion panel fills as you read. Trust-led, lowest bait-and-switch risk.

Why it wins: highest trust + brand fit. Watch: long-form can bury the builder — companion travels the full scroll, one-tap add per chapter.
Open prototype
6Section lab

Reels Lab

Founder video × products

Six working section prototypes pairing the 5-step regimen with Emily's social reels — Reel Rail, Story Mode, Split Stage, Founder's Shelf, Routine Timeline, and Proof Pairing.

What it's for: compare ways to fold founder video into any of the five concepts. Watch: simulated playback — no real footage yet.
