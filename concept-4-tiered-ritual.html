It started on a mountain, with a question about my own skin.

"I wanted something honest — that treated aging skin with respect, not panic. Mountain Glow was the first thing I made that I actually felt in the mirror."

Emily Doyle started Ursa Major in a small Vermont workshop, formulating in batches by hand. Mountain Glow · what it does

A treatment serum, built for skin that's lived.

Cold-pressed botanical oils and a quiet dose of actives, balanced for mature and maturing skin. It works on five things at once — slowly, the way real skin changes.

  • FirmnessSupports a firmer, more lifted look at the cheek and jaw.
